Megan McCormick - President

Megan has been a part of SCNO for four semesters and is currently serving as president. She will be a part of the class of 2019 at the University of Pittsburgh and is studying Industrial Engineering and Sociology. Megan is also involved in research through the Swanson School of Engineering and is a chair member of the professional engineering fraternity Theta Tau. Citing SCNO as the beginning of her interest in the subject, Megan hopes to pursue consulting post-graduation. The application of efficient business practices to the work of our many inspiring clients combines her passion for service with her enthusiasm for high-level problem solving, and highlights why Megan is immensely proud to be a part of SCNO.
